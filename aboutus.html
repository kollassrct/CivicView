Our Story

Building India's Digital
Civic Infrastructure

CIVICVIEW is revolutionizing how Indian citizens connect with local government, making civic engagement transparent, efficient, and accessible to everyone across the country.

Our Mission

To empower every Indian citizen with a direct voice in improving their communities through technology-driven civic engagement and transparent governance.

Our Vision

A digitally connected India where every civic issue is heard, tracked, and resolved efficiently, creating cleaner, safer, and more livable communities for all.

Platform Capabilities

Comprehensive tools for modern civic engagement across Indian cities

Real-time Issue Reporting

Citizens can instantly report civic issues with photos, location data, and detailed descriptions through our mobile-first platform.

Transparent Tracking

Every reported issue includes real-time status updates, allowing citizens to track progress from submission to resolution.

Community Engagement

Foster neighborhood collaboration through upvoting, commenting, and sharing civic issues to amplify community voices.

Smart Prioritization

AI-powered algorithms help municipal authorities prioritize issues based on impact, urgency, and community feedback.

Government Dashboard

Comprehensive administrative tools for municipal corporations to manage, assign, and track issue resolution efficiently.

Impact Analytics

Detailed reporting and analytics help measure civic improvement impact and guide policy decisions across regions.
