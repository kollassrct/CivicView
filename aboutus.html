About CIVICVIEW

Making Indian Cities Better Together

CIVICVIEW is a technology-driven platform that empowers citizens to report civic issues and work together with local authorities to create positive change in their communities.

Our Mission

We believe that every citizen has the power to make a difference in their community. CIVICVIEW bridges the gap between citizens and local authorities, creating a transparent, efficient, and collaborative approach to solving civic issues.

From potholes to streetlight issues, from garbage collection problems to water logging, we make it easy for citizens to report problems and track their resolution in real-time.

Transparency

Real-time tracking and updates ensure complete transparency in the issue resolution process.

Community

Building stronger communities through collaborative civic engagement and shared responsibility.

Efficiency

Streamlined reporting and response processes that save time and resources for everyone.

How It Works

Simple Steps to Make Change

Reporting civic issues and tracking their resolution has never been easier.

1

Report the Issue

Take a photo of the civic issue, add location details, and describe the problem in a few simple steps.

2

3

Track Progress

Monitor the status of your report in real-time and get notified when the issue is resolved.
