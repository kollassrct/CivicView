Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about CIVICVIEW.

How do I report a civic issue?

Simply create a citizen account, click on "Report an Issue", fill in the details with a photo and location, and submit. Your report will be automatically sent to the relevant authorities.

How long does it take for issues to be resolved?

Resolution time varies depending on the type and severity of the issue. You can track the progress of your reported issues in real-time through your citizen dashboard.

Is CIVICVIEW available in my city?

CIVICVIEW is currently available in 45+ cities across India and expanding rapidly. Contact us if you'd like to bring CIVICVIEW to your city.

Can I report issues anonymously?

While we encourage users to create accounts for better tracking and communication, we do support anonymous reporting for sensitive issues. However, account-based reporting allows for better follow-up and updates.
