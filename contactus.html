Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How quickly do you respond to civic reports?

Most civic reports are acknowledged within 24-48 hours. Resolution times vary based on the complexity and priority of the issue, but we provide real-time updates throughout the process.

Is CIVICVIEW available in my city?

CIVICVIEW is currently active in over 100 cities across India. Check our platform to see if your city is listed, or contact us to discuss bringing CIVICVIEW to your area.

How can municipal authorities join the platform?

Municipal corporations and government authorities can contact us through the partnership inquiry option. We provide comprehensive onboarding and training for administrative teams.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we take data privacy seriously. All personal information is encrypted and stored securely. We comply with Indian data protection regulations and never share your data without consent.
